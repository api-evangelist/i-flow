# I Flow (i-flow)

iFlow Inc. is an energy and water solutions provider headquartered in Santa Ana, California, offering products and services for the sustainable use of vital resources including water metering and leak detection, wastewater management, gas measurement and control, thermal energy/BTU metering, and data connectivity solutions.
